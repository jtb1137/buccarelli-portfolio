About Me

Who am I?

My name is Jake Buccarelli. I build websites and full-stack applications. A northern New Jersey native, I now call Philadelphia my home. Following my graduation from Penn State in 2016, I began my professional life in pharmaceutical advertising. However, as a lifelong techie, I made a drastic shift in my career path. I decided to learn how to code.

A year later, I'm working freelance as a Web Developer, doing what I love, in an industry that rewards passion, curiosity, and creativity. I'm learning something new every day, and I can't wait to see what new people, projects, and technologies I'm introduced to as I continue on in my programming career.
